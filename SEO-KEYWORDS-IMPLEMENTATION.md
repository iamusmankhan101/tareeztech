## SEO Best Practices Applied

### ✅ Natural Keyword Integration
- Keywords are integrated naturally within sentences
- No keyword stuffing
- Maintains readability and user experience

### ✅ Keyword Density
- Primary keywords appear 3-5 times throughout the page
- Secondary keywords appear 1-3 times
- Long-tail keywords appear at least once

### ✅ Strategic Placement
- Keywords in H1 tags (Hero heading)
- Keywords in meta title and description
- Keywords in navigation and footer
- Keywords in service descriptions
- Keywords in animated text sections

### ✅ Semantic SEO
- Related terms and synonyms used together
- Contextual relevance maintained
- Topic clustering around "digital marketing" and "web development"

### ✅ User Intent Matching
- Informational keywords: "what is a digital marketing agency", "advantages of digital marketing"
- Commercial keywords: "best web development company", "top digital marketing agency"
- Transactional keywords: "custom web development services", "web app development services"
- Local keywords: "in Lahore", "near me", "in Pakistan"
